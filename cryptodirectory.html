Wallets

Desktop Wallets

Bitcoin Only

Electrum

A lightweight and widely used Bitcoin only wallet for desktop users.

Multichain

Exodus

A beginner friendly wallet supporting Bitcoin, Ethereum, and many other assets.

Atomic Wallet

A multichain desktop wallet with built in swapping features.

Browser Wallets

MetaMask

The most commonly used browser wallet for Ethereum and Ethereum based chains.

Trust Wallet

A popular wallet available as a browser extension and mobile app.

Rabby

A browser wallet focused on safety warnings and transaction clarity.

Most wallets also offer mobile apps.

Hardware Wallets

Trezor

A well known hardware wallet for long term secure storage.

Ledger

A popular hardware wallet supporting a wide range of assets.

Tangem

A card based hardware wallet designed for simplicity.

TMX Global

A hardware wallet solution with additional enterprise focused features.

Important:
When purchasing a hardware wallet, always order directly from the manufacturer. Do not buy from third party marketplaces such as Amazon or Newegg.

Centralized Exchanges

Gemini

A regulated exchange based in the US. Not related to Google Gemini.

Kraken

A long standing exchange known for security and transparency.

Crypto.com

An exchange offering trading, cards, and other crypto services.

Binance

One of the largest global cryptocurrency exchanges.

Bisq

Peer to peer cryptocurrency trading without traditional accounts. Not recommended for novice users.

Swap Tools

FixedFloat

A quick swap service for exchanging cryptocurrencies. Not available in the US.

SimpleSwap

A beginner friendly crypto swap service. Creating an account isn't required, but helps save on fees.

SideShift

A privacy focused instant exchange service.

Decentralized Exchanges

For use with Web3 wallets such as MetaMask or Trust Wallet.

Uniswap

The primary decentralized exchange on Ethereum.

SushiSwap

An alternative decentralized exchange on Ethereum.

Raydium

The most commonly used DEX on the Solana network.

PancakeSwap

The primary DEX for Binance Smart Chain.

Smol Refuel

A gasless swap utility for when you do not have the native gas token for a chain.

Charts and Market Data

CoinMarketCap

Market data, rankings, and basic token information.

CoinGecko

Comprehensive cryptocurrency market data and analytics.

Crypto Backed Debit Cards

OnyxCard Info OnyxCard Sign Up

A crypto backed debit card. The info page explains the program and the signup page allows you to apply.

Bancus

A crypto friendly payment card solution.

UltimoPay

A crypto backed card and payment platform.

Where to Use Your Crypto

Bitrefill

Gift cards and mobile service top ups using crypto.

Stealths

Similar to Bitrefill with a focus on privacy.

Mullvad

A privacy focused VPN service accepting crypto.

ShopinBit

The Giving Block

NanoGPT
