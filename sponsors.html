Become a Sponsor

Sponsoring the Toolbox is a direct contribution to the infrastructure many in this community rely on — wallet setup guides, the approval scanner, the calculator, the RPC switcher, and more. All of it is built and maintained by one person, independently.

Sponsorships are paid in JOY, one of our community tokens. This is a deliberate choice — it keeps sponsorship native to the ecosystem and creates another use case for JOY as the marketplace launches.

What sponsors receive

  • ✓ The knowledge that the tools you rely on stay live and actively maintained
  • ✓ Optional short note or tagline alongside your listing (your choice)
