🔐 Key Takeaways

  • Never enter your Secret Recovery Phrase on any website.
  • MetaMask will never ask for your phrase via a link or email.
  • Check the URL bar carefully — one wrong character = phishing site.
  • No alert, urgency, or "verification" can ever justify sharing your phrase.
  • When in doubt, close the tab and go directly to metamask.io.
