Add Tokens to MetaMask

Copy a contract address, then add it as a custom token in MetaMask.

How to add in MetaMask Mobile

  1. Copy the token contract address below.
  2. Tap the back arrow in the top left corner.
  3. Tap Home in the bottom left corner.
  4. Tap the plus sign above BNB.
  5. Tap Custom token.
  6. Paste the contract address.
  7. Tap Next, then Import.
  8. Repeat for each token.
